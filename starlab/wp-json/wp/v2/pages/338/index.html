What is a System-of-Systems (SoS)? and Why Is SoS Engineering Needed?<\/strong><\/h4>\n

A System-of-Systems (SoS) is a set or arrangement of constituent systems (CSs) that are integrated (i.e., related or connected) into a larger system that delivers higher-level unique capabilities [DoD, JCIDS]<\/p>\n

    \n
  • An SoS consists of CSs, which have managerial independence and operational independence, and they cooperate with each other to achieve SoS-level goals [M. W. Maier]<\/li>\n
  • A set of several independently acquired systems (i.e., CSs), which are independent and form in their combined operation a multifunctional solution to an overall coherent mission [H. Eisner et al.]<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Our Approach for Model-based SoS Engineering<\/strong><\/h4>\n

    <\/p>\n

     <\/p>\n

      \n
    • For modeling and specifications of SoS, we study <Model-based SoS Analysis & Design><\/strong>. This study includes following main research topics:\n
        \n
      • Ontology-based Meta-modeling of SoS<\/li>\n
      • SoS Modeling Method<\/li>\n
      • Data-based(or -driven) SoS Environment Analysis and Modeling<\/li>\n
      • Policy Modeling & Specifications<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n
          \n
        • For verification and validation of SoS, we study <Model-based SoS V&V and Simulation><\/strong>. This\u00a0study\u00a0includes following main research topics:\n
            \n
          • Discrete Event Simulation<\/li>\n
          • Agent-based Simulation<\/li>\n
          • Simulation-based Statistical Model Checking<\/li>\n
          • Property-based SoS Model Slicing (Goal Model Slicing, Simulation Model Slicing)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

            OpenSource SW Development<\/strong><\/h3>\n

            SIMVA-SoS (Simulation-based Verification & Analysis for SoS) is a software for simulating and verifying the SoS, which is under development. Three main techniques\/components are integrated into or utilized by SIMVA-SoS for simulation-based verification and analysis of SoS.<\/p>\n
